Ground Beef Lasagna With Pastry Dough

Home

Description

Lasagna is a dish that everyone loves, and when it’s practical to make, it becomes even better for busy everyday life. To help with that, we’ve prepared a ground beef lasagna recipe made with pastry dough that can be ready in just 45 minutes and serves up to 10 people. Be sure to check all the details below:

Ingredients

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1.5 kg of ground beef
  • 1.5 kg of crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of chopped parsley
  • 1 roll of pastry dough (500g)
  • 400g of sliced ​​mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil for greasing
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, heat the oil and sauté the onion and garlic.
  2. Add the ground beef and fry for 5 minutes or until browned.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and sauté for another 10 minutes over low heat.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, turn off the heat, and stir in the parsley.
  5. In a greased medium-sized baking dish, unroll the dough and then place a layer of dough, but do not cut the dough.
  6. Place a portion of the meat and a portion of mozzarella.
  7. Place the pastry dough over the filling and continue making layers, going back and forth with the dough and filling, finishing with sauce and cheese.
  8. Seal the edges with wooden sticks.
  9. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in a preheated medium oven for 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
  10. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
