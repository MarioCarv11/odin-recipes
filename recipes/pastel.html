Chicken and Cheese Pastel

Home

Description

Chicken and cheese pastel is a popular Brazilian street-food snack made with thin pastry dough filled with seasoned shredded chicken and melted cheese. The pastry is folded, sealed, and deep-fried until golden and crispy, creating a crunchy exterior with a warm, savory filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the shredded chicken and tomato sauce.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly, then mix in the mozzarella cheese.
  6. Place a sheet of pastel dough on a clean surface.
  7. Add 2–3 tablespoons of filling to one side.
  8. Fold the dough over to form a half-moon or rectangle.
  9. Press the edges with a fork to seal.
  10. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to about 350°F (180°C).
  11. Fry the pastéis for 2–3 minutes per side until golden and crispy.
  12. Remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
