Condensed milk Pudding

Home

Description

Condensed milk pudding is a classic dessert made with condensed milk, eggs, and milk, baked in a caramel-coated mold using a water bath. The mixture is blended, poured over homemade caramel, and baked until set, creating a smooth and creamy texture. After chilling, the pudding is unmolded so the caramel sauce flows over the top, giving it a rich, sweet flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz / 395 g) condensed milk
  • 2 cups whole milk (about the same volume as the condensed milk can ×2)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup hot water

Steps

  1. Check if you have all the ingredients and get ready to make the best condensed milk pudding, which comes out smooth and without holes;
  2. To make the syrup, choose a heavy-bottomed saucepan and add the sugar. Turn the heat to low and melt until there are almost no sugar crystals left.
  3. Carefully pour the hot water into the pan and stir vigorously to melt the sugar crystals again, or until the sugar lumps dissolve and form a syrup-like consistency.
  4. Pour the syrup into a 19 cm diameter pudding mold and spread it to cover the bottom and sides. Set aside while you prepare the pudding.
  5. Crack one egg at a time into a separate bowl and, if it's good, add it to the blender. Add the milk and condensed milk and blend for approximately 1 minute until the mixture is completely smooth.
  6. Place a sieve over the mold with the caramel and pour the pudding mixture over it. This will help remove bubbles and any other bits of ingredients that weren't blended, making it even smoother.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 180ºC for approximately 45 minutes in a water bath. Simply place the pudding mold in a rectangular baking pan with high sides filled with water;
  8. Then, remove from the oven, let the pudding cool slightly, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  9. Run a knife along the center and sides of the mold to loosen the pudding. If necessary, place the mold over a stovetop burner to quickly melt the caramel and easily unmold the pudding. Unmold onto a large plate or cake stand. Serve and enjoy!
